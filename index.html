Demographic Data Lake & Analytics Warehouse (AWS + SQL)

I designed and built a cloud-native demographic data lake on AWS that ingests population and fertility data from the World Bank and UN datasets, transforms it using SQL-based ELT pipelines, and models it into a dimesnional warehouse for demographic analytics.

The system supports population growth analysis, fertility trends, and country-level demographic comparisons using a star schema optimized for analytical queries.

Tech Stack:

  • Data Ingestion: AWS Glue for orchestrating ETL pipelines, AWS S3 for scalable data storage.
  • Data Transformation: SQL-based transformations using AWS Athena and Redshift Spectrum for serverless querying and data modeling.
  • Data Modeling: Dimensional modeling with star schema design to optimize query performance for demographic analytics.
  • Analytics: AWS QuickSight for interactive dashboards and visualizations of demographic trends and insights.
  • Data Governance: AWS Lake Formation for managing data access and ensuring compliance with data security standards.
  • API ingestion:Python

About

I am an actuarial-trained data engineer focused on strengthening the infrastructure behind demographic and health intelligence systems

My background in actuarial science equipped me with expertise in mortality modeling, survival analysis, and quantitative risk evaluation. As I worked more closely with real-worl datasets, I recognized that sustainable impact depends on the quality, scalability, and governance of the underlying data systems. This realization led me to specialize in data engineering. Building reliable, cloud-native platforms that transform complex, multi-source population and health data into structured, analytics-ready assets.

I design and implement AWS-based data lakes and warehouses using SQL-centered ELT frameworks, dimensional modeling principles, and dara quality validation techniques. My work emphasizes reproductivity, performance optimization, and long-term maintainablity, ensuring that demographic indicators such as fertility rates, mortality metrics, and maternal health stastics are engineered to support evidence-based planning and policy implementation.

I am particularly motivated by strengthening data systems that underpin population analytics, reproductive health monitoring, and Sustainable Development Goal reporting. I believe resilient data infrastructure is essential for effective resource allocation, equitable service delivery, and informed decision-making withing global development institutions.

Beyong my technical work, I am the founder of a Data and AI communitythat brings together data professionals, students and practitioners through technical meetups and hackathons. The community focuses on practical skill development, collaborative problem-solving, and building production-ready data solutions that address real-world challenges. Through this initiative, I aim to contribute to a stronger, more capable data ecosystem withing the region.

My long-term objective is to contribute to global development organizations by engineering scalable, trustworthy demographic data platforms that enhance analytical capacity and strengthen institutional decision-making.
