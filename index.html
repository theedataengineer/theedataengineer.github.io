Demographic Data Lake & Analytics Warehouse (AWS + SQL)

I designed and built a cloud-native demographic data lake on AWS that ingests population and fertility data from the World Bank and UN datasets, transforms it using SQL-based ELT pipelines, and models it into a dimesnional warehouse for demographic analytics.

The system supports population growth analysis, fertility trends, and country-level demographic comparisons using a star schema optimized for analytical queries.

Tech Stack:

  • Data Ingestion: AWS Glue for orchestrating ETL pipelines, AWS S3 for scalable data storage.
  • Data Transformation: SQL-based transformations using AWS Athena and Redshift Spectrum for serverless querying and data modeling.
  • Data Modeling: Dimensional modeling with star schema design to optimize query performance for demographic analytics.
  • Analytics: AWS QuickSight for interactive dashboards and visualizations of demographic trends and insights.
  • Data Governance: AWS Lake Formation for managing data access and ensuring compliance with data security standards.
  • API ingestion:Python

About

I am an actuarial-trained data engineer passionate about building reliable data systems that power demographic and health intelligence

My journey into data engineering began with a deep curiosity about risk, how populations change, how mortality trends evolve, and how data can influence policy decisions that affect millions of lives. With a foundation in actuarial science, I developed strong quantitative reasoning and deep appreciation for structured, precise modeling. Today, I apply that mindset to designing scalable data platforms in the cloud.

I specialize in building cloud-based data lakes and data warehouses, writing advanced SQL transformations, and engineerin ELT/ETL pipelines that transform raw population, fertility,and health datasets into analytics-ready systems. I am particularly drawn to demographic and reproductive health data, areas where strong data infrastructure directly supports evidence-based policy and sustainable development.

Beyond my technical work, I founded a Data and AI community committed to connecting and helping data professionals build practical, production-ready skills. I believe data is not just about algorithms or dashboards. It is about responsibility, accessibility, and long-term impact.

My long-term goal is to contribute to global development institutions by engineering resilient, trustworthy data systems that strengthen population analytics, health reporting frameworks, and decision-making processes.

At the intersection of actuarial thinking and modern data engineering, I am building systems that turn complex demographic data into structured intelligence.
